          <h1>Properties</h1>
<p><em>Minimum access level</em>: <code>Tax, Accounting &amp; Users</code></p>

<p>Only applicable for companies of type <code>UkUnincorporatedLandlord</code>. Other company types cannot have or create properties.</p>
