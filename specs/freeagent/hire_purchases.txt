          <h1>Hire purchases</h1>
<p><em>Only available to UK companies</em></p>

<p><em>Minimum access level</em>: <code>Bills</code></p>

<p>Hire purchases can be created or removed by passing the relevant attribute to the <a href="bills.html">Bills API</a></p>
