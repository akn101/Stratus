          <h1>Cashflow</h1>
<p><em>Minimum access level</em>: <code>Banking</code>, unless stated otherwise.</p>

<p>Provides read-only access to historic cashflow data generated within FreeAgent. As Projected cashflow is not returned, requests with dates in the future will return a <code>total</code> of <code>0</code>.</p>
<h2 id="cashflow-summary-for-a-given-date-range">Cashflow summary for a given date range</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/cashflow?from_date=2019-07-01&amp;to_date=2019-09-30
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>from_date</code> - Date in YYYY-MM-DD format</li>
<li><code>to_date</code> - Date in YYYY-MM-DD format</li>
</ul>
