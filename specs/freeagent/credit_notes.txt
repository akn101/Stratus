<h2 id="list-all-credit-notes">List all credit notes</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/credit_notes
</code></pre><h3>Input</h3><h4>View Filters</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/credit_notes?view=recent_open_or_overdue
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>all</code>: Show all credit notes (default)</li>
<li><code>recent_open_or_overdue</code>: Show only recent, open, or overdue invoices.</li>
<li><code>open</code>: Show only open credit notes.</li>
<li><code>overdue</code>: Show only overdue credit notes.</li>
<li><code>open_or_overdue</code>: Show only open or overdue credit notes.</li>
<li><code>draft</code>: Show only draft credit notes.</li>
<li><code>refunded</code>: Show only refunded credit notes.</li>
<li><code>last_N_months</code>: Show only credit notes from the last <code>N</code> months.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Date Filters</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/credit_notes?updated_since=2017-05-22T09:00:00.000Z
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>updated_since</code></li>
</ul>
<h4>Sort Orders</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/credit_notes?sort=updated_at
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>created_at</code>: Sort by the time the credit note was created (default).</li>
<li><code>updated_at</code>: Sort by the time the credit note was last modified.</li>
</ul>

<p>To sort in descending order, the sort parameter can be prefixed with a hyphen.</p>
<pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/credit_notes?sort=-updated_at
