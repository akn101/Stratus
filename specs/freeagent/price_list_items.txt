<h2 id="list-all-price-list-items">List all price list items</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/price_list_items
</code></pre><h4>Sort Orders</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/price_list_items?sort=created_at
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>created_at</code>: Sort by the time the price list item was created (default).</li>
<li><code>code</code>: Sort by the price list item code.</li>
<li><code>updated_at</code>: Sort by the time the price list item was updated.</li>
</ul>

<p>To sort in descending order, the sort parameter can be prefixed with a hyphen.</p>
<pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/price_list_items?sort=-created_at
