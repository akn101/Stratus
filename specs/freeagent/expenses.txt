<h2 id="list-all-expenses">List all expenses</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/expenses
</code></pre><h3>Input</h3><h4>View Filters</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/expenses?view=recent
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>recent</code>: Show only recent expenses.</li>
<li><code>recurring</code>: Show recurring expenses.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Date Filters</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/expenses?from_date=2012-01-01&amp;to_date=2012-03-31
</code></pre><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/expenses?updated_since=2017-05-22T09:00:00.000Z
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>from_date</code></li>
<li><code>to_date</code></li>
<li><code>updated_since</code></li>
</ul>
<h4>Project Filters</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/expenses?project=https://api.freeagent.com/v2/projects/2
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>project</code>: show only expenses related to this project</li>
</ul>
