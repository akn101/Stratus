<h2 id="get-the-p-amp-l-summary">Get the P&amp;L summary</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/accounting/profit_and_loss/summary
</code></pre><h4>Date Filters</h4>
<p><em>Note:</em> Requested date periods must be equal to or less than 12 months or be contained within a single accounting year.</p>

<p>The request can be filtered either with an annual accounting period or by specifying start and end dates.
When no parameters are provided the default time period will be the current accounting year
to date.</p>

<ul>
<li><code>from_date</code></li>
<li><code>to_date</code></li>
</ul>

<p>The <code>from_date</code> and <code>to_date</code> params should be in the format <code>YYYY-MM-DD</code>. If specifying only one of the date params,
the other will default: <code>from_date</code> to the start of the current accounting period and <code>to_date</code> to today.</p>

<ul>
<li><code>accounting_period</code></li>
</ul>

<p>The accounting period parameter should be in the format <code>2022/23</code>, indicating the start and end year of the period.</p>
