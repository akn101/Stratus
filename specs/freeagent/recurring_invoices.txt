          <h1>Recurring Invoices</h1>
<p><em>Minimum access level</em>: <code>Estimates and Invoices</code>, unless stated otherwise.</p>
<h2 id="attributes">Attributes</h2>
<p>The attributes below are in addition to the <a href="invoices.html#invoice-attributes">Invoice Attributes</a>.</p>

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Attribute</th>
      <th>Description</th>
      <th>Kind</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>

  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>url</td>
      <td>The unique identifier for the recurring invoice</td>
      <td>URI</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>frequency</td>
      <td>
        One of the following:
        <p>
          <ul>
            <li><code>Weekly</code></li>
            <li><code>Two Weekly</code></li>
            <li><code>Four Weekly</code></li>
            <li><code>Monthly</code></li>
            <li><code>Two Monthly</code></li>
            <li><code>Quarterly</code></li>
            <li><code>Biannually</code></li>
            <li><code>Annually</code></li>
            <li><code>2-Yearly</code></li>
          </ul>
        </p>
      </td>
      <td>String</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>recurring_status</td>
      <td>
        One of the following:
        <p>
          <ul>
            <li><code>Draft</code></li>
            <li><code>Active</code></li>
          </ul>
        </p>
      </td>
      <td>String</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>recurring_end_date</td>
      <td>
        <p>When the recurring ends, in <code>YYYY-MM-DD</code> format</p>
        <em>Blank if recurring forever</em>
      </td>
      <td>Date</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>next_recurs_on</td>
      <td>When the invoice recurs next, in <code>YYYY-MM-DD</code> format</td>
      <td>Date</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
<h2 id="list-all-recurring-invoices">List all recurring invoices</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/recurring_invoices
</code></pre><h3>Input</h3><h4>View Filters</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/recurring_invoices?view=draft
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>draft</code>: Show only draft recurring invoices.</li>
<li><code>active</code>: Show only active recurring invoices.</li>
<li><code>inactive</code>: Show only inactive recurring invoices.</li>
</ul>
