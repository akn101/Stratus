          <h1>Construction Industry Scheme Bands</h1>
<p><em>Only available to UK companies enrolled in the Construction Industry Scheme for Subcontractors</em></p>

<p><em>Minimum access level</em>:</p>

<ul>
<li><code>Estimates &amp; Invoices</code></li>
</ul>
<h2 id="list-all-cis-bands-for-a-company">List all CIS bands for a company</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/cis_bands
</code></pre>
<p>These are the Construction Industry Scheme bands that have been selected for the company in the
CIS for Subcontractors settings.</p>

<p>Since these bands do not change frequently, we suggest caching them client-side and updating
every once in a while, for example once a day or at app start-up. Use the names of the bands in
conjunction with the <code>cis_rate</code> attribute on invoices.</p>
