          <h1>Sales Tax Periods</h1>
<p><em>Only available to US and Universal companies</em></p>

<p><em>Minimum access level</em>:</p>

<ul>
<li><code>Estimates &amp; Invoices</code> to <strong>read</strong></li>
<li><code>Full Access</code> to <strong>create, update</strong>, or <strong>delete</strong></li>
</ul>
<h2 id="attributes">Attributes</h2>
<p>Attributes marked <em>Required</em> should be present in the request to create new
sales tax periods.</p>

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Required</th>
      <th>Attribute</th>
      <th>Description</th>
      <th>Kind</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>url</td>
      <td>The unique identifier for the sales tax period</td>
      <td>URI</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>&#10004;</td>
      <td>sales_tax_name</td>
      <td>Name of <a href="sales_tax.html">sales tax</a> e.g. VAT, GST, Sales Tax</td>
      <td>String</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>&#10004;</td>
      <td>sales_tax_registration_status</td>
      <td>
        One of the following:
        <p>
          <ul>
            <li><code>Not Registered</code></li>
            <li><code>Registered</code></li>
          </ul>
        </p>
      </td>
      <td>String</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>&#10004;</td>
      <td>sales_tax_rate_1</td>
      <td>First rate of the <a href="sales_tax.html">sales tax</a></td>
      <td>Decimal</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>sales_tax_rate_2</td>
      <td>Second rate of the <a href="sales_tax.html">sales tax</a></td>
      <td>Decimal</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>sales_tax_rate_3</td>
      <td>Third rate of the <a href="sales_tax.html">sales tax</a></td>
      <td>Decimal</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>&#10004;</td>
      <td>sales_tax_is_value_added</td>
      <td><code>true</code> if you can reclaim tax on what you buy, <code>false</code> otherwise</td>
      <td>Boolean</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>sales_tax_registration_number</td>
      <td>
        Do not specify if no registration number is required
      </td>
      <td>String</td>
    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td>&#10004;</td>
      <td>effective_date</td>
      <td>When the sales tax period takes effect, in <code>YYYY-MM-DD</code> format</td>
      <td>Date</td>
    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>is_locked</td>
      <td><code>true</code> if no changes can be made, <code>false</code> otherwise</td>
      <td>Boolean</td>
    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>locked_reason</td>
      <td>The reason for the sales tax period being locked</td>
      <td>Boolean</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

<p><br /></p>
<h4>Extra attributes for Universal companies</h4>
<p>All attributes displayed below are optional.</p>

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Attribute</th>
      <th>Description</th>
      <th>Kind</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>

  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>second_sales_tax_name</td>
      <td>
        <p>Name of <a href="sales_tax.html">second sales tax</a> (e.g. PST in some Canadian provinces)</p>
        <em>Do not specify if no second Sales Tax is to be charged</em>
      </td>
      <td>String</td>
    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td>second_sales_tax_rate_1</td>
      <td>First rate of the <a href="sales_tax.html">second sales tax</a></td>
      <td>Decimal</td>
    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td>second_sales_tax_rate_2</td>
      <td>Second rate of the <a href="sales_tax.html">second sales tax</a></td>
      <td>Decimal</td>
    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td>second_sales_tax_rate_3</td>
      <td>Third rate of the <a href="sales_tax.html">second sales tax</a></td>
      <td>Decimal</td>
    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td>second_sales_tax_is_compound</td>
      <td>
        <code>true</code> if applied on top of the main sales tax instead of independently, <code>false</code> otherwise
      </td>
      <td>Boolean</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
