          <h1>Final Accounts Reports</h1>
<p><em>Minimum access level</em>: <code>Tax, Accounting &amp; Users</code>, unless stated otherwise.</p>

<p>This endpoint allows API applications to retrieve information on a company&#39;s Final Accounts Reports.</p>

<td>The end date of the period</td>
<td>Date</td>
</tr>
