          <h1>Notes</h1>
<p><em>Minimum access level</em> <code>Contacts and Projects</code>, unless stated otherwise.</p>

<p>Contacts and Projects can both have Notes.</p>
<h2 id="attributes">Attributes</h2>
<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Required</th>
      <th>Attribute</th>
      <th>Description</th>

    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>url</td>
      <td>The unique identifier for the note</td>
      <td>URI</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>&#10004;</td>
      <td>note</td>
      <td>The content of the note</td>
      <td>String</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <td>parent_url</td>

      <td>URI</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
