<h2 id="list-all-bills">List all bills</h2><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/bills
</code></pre><h3>Input</h3><h4>View Filters</h4><pre class=prettyprint><code class=>GET https://api.freeagent.com/v2/bills?view=open
</code></pre>
<ul>
<li><code>all</code>: (default)</li>
<li><code>open</code>: Show only open bills.</li>
<li><code>overdue</code>: Show only overdue bills.</li>
<li><code>open_or_overdue</code>: Show only open or overdue bills.</li>
<li><code>open_or_overdue_payments</code>: Show only open or overdue bill payments (bills with positive total value).</li>
<li><code>open_or_overdue_refunds</code>: Show only open or overdue bill refunds (bills with negative total value).</li>
<li><code>paid</code>: Show only paid bills.</li>
<li><code>recurring</code>: Show only recurring bills.</li>
<li><code>hire_purchase</code>: Show only hire purchase bills.</li>
<li><code>cis</code>: Show only bills with any CIS bill items.</li>
</ul>
