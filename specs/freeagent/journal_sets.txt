          <h1>Journal Sets</h1>
<p><em>Minimum access level</em>: <code>Tax, Accounting and Users</code>, unless stated otherwise.</p>

<p>FreeAgent models journalled corrections to accounts in &#39;sets&#39; - collections of journal entries, all on the same day, which must balance to be valid.</p>
